Reviewing Puzzles

The review page replays the moves you made in a puzzle, showing you how each move is implied or if it is in error. You may also pause the replay at any point, and then explore the moves implied at that point.

Playback

  • Play resumes replaying your moves.
  • Back plays your moves backwards.
  • Pause stops playback, and starts calculating all the implied moves at the stopping point.
  • Redo goes forward one move.
  • Undo goes back one move.
You may also jump to any point in the playback using the slider below these buttons.

Exploration

When playback is paused, the moves implied by the current state of the grid are calculated. Squares with implied moves are outlined in green (for simple moves) to yellow (for complicated moves or series of moves).
