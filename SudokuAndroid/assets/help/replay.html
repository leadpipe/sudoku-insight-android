Reviewing Puzzles

The review page replays the moves you made in a puzzle, showing you how each move is implied or if it is in error. You may also pause the replay at any point, and then explore the moves implied at that point.

Playback

  • Pause stops playback, and starts calculating all the implied moves at the stopping point.
  • Reverse and Play resume replaying your moves, backwards or forwards.
  • Undo and Redo go backward or forward one move.
  • The slider below these buttons lets you jump to any point in the playback.

Exploration

When playback is paused, the app calculates insights implied by the current state of the grid. You can touch an outlined square to see the insights associated with that square.
  • Yellow/green outline: an assignment is implied for that location. Darker green means a shorter chain of insights leading to the assignment, lighter means a longer chain. Yellow means the number of links hasn't been calculated yet.
  • Orange outline: a disproof is available at that location. This is a proposed assignment to that location and the error that would result, with a chain of insights between them.
During exploration, these actions are enabled:
  • Apply makes the selected assignment, or crosses out the disproved assignment. Once you've applied one, every location you select automatically applies its insight.
  • Clear clears the insights from the grid.
  • Resume undoes any applied insights and resumes playing back your moves.
  • Undo and Redo also work while exploring.
