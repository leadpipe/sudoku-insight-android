Settings

  • When you share your anonymous data, the moves you make to solve puzzles become publicly available at sudoku-insight.appspot.com. However, no identifying information about you is associated with those moves.
  • If you set up an account, then you can install Sudoku Insight on more than one device and be sure to get a different stream of puzzles on each one. (You may get one or two repeated just after you set up the account on the new device.) Your account will only be used to coordinate among multiple installations of Sudoku Insight.
  • Most Sudokus in the world are so-called "proper," meaning they have just one solution. If you're up for a challenge, let the app show you the occasional "improper" puzzle.
