Entering or Capturing Puzzles

To capture a Sudoku using the camera:
  • Install the Google Goggles app.
  • Take a picture of the puzzle using Goggles.
  • When it lists Sudoku Puzzle as a recognized item, tap it.
  • On the following screen, tap Solve.
  • Complete the action using Sudoku Insight.
In the text box, say where the puzzle comes from: for example, "Chicago Tribune" or "Sleek and Smart Sudoku". This text may be used to publicly describe the puzzle on sudoku-insight.appspot.com, so to maintain your anonymity please keep it generic.

When entering a Sudoku by hand, the buttons to Play Now or Save for Later will only become enabled when enough clues are entered to make it a valid puzzle.
